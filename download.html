In the game, players take on the role of young graffiti artists who are caught in the act of tagging a metro railway site. The main characters, Jake, Tricky, and Fresh, then run along the subway tracks to escape from the grumpy Inspector and his dog. Players navigate the characters through various urban landscapes, including subway tracks, tunnels, and rooftops, while collecting coins and power-ups along the way. The goal is to run as far as possible, avoiding obstacles such as trains, barriers, and incoming trains. Players can also perform acrobatic moves like jumping, rolling, and sliding to dodge obstacles and oncoming trains.
